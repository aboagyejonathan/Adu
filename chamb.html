    CHAMBERED NAUTILUS ALUMNI

    SCI HIGH ALUMNI ARE PART OF THE GREATEST NETWORK OF SCIENTISTS, TECHNOLOGY PROFESSIONALS, ENGINEERS, AND MATHEMATICIANS REPRESENTING NEW ORLEANS ACROSS THE GLOBE.

    The New Orleans Charter Science & Mathematics High School is committed to connecting our community after high school. We invite alumni to visit the campus and join us alongside our mission by helping to organize annual events and fundraisers. You can connect with Sci High on Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter and by updating your contact information to receive our email newsletter, and other updates.

    Please feel free to contact us with any questions you may have, thoughts about how to enhance our alumni program, or to schedule a tour to see our brand new, state-of-the-art facility.
